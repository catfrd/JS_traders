Our Story

The founder and proprietor of M/s J S Traders was late Mr. N G A AHAMED MEERA SAHIB, one of the pioneers in the steel trading industry. Founded in the year 1984 and started the trade business in the field of Iron & Steel industry, which traded products like CTD bars, M.S. Wires, M.S. Rods and other structural products.

Mr. A AHAMED ASHRAF the son of Mr. N G A AHAMED MEERA SAHIB took over the management in the year 2009 as Proprietor. Since then, the company has excelled in the sector of Iron & Steel trading throughout Tamil Nadu & Pondicherry for the past decades. The primitive focus of J S Traders shifted to TMT bars, Cements & Structural products catering the Trade sector and Building & Construction Industry under the guidance of Mr. A AHAMED ASHRAF.

During October 2022 M/s J S Traders is now registered as a Partnership firm by appointing Mr. A AHAMED ASHRAF its Managing Partner and his wife Mrs. A MINNATHUL MISIRIA as its working Partner and this partnership is now became as a whole sale distributor and dealer enterprise dealing with top brands in the Iron & steel sector in Tamil Nadu.
Our portfolio includes the service of supplying the leading real estate developers and construction companies in Tamil Nadu. We also have a wide range of supply chain network throughout Tamil Nadu & Pondicherry catering about 500 dealers and sub dealers.

About Us


Established in 1984, J S Traders has been a trailblazer in the distribution of steel products and construction materials throughout Tamil Nadu and Pondicherry. With nearly four decades of experience, the company has earned a stellar reputation for its unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. J S Traders boasts a comprehensive product range, including structural steel, TMT bars, steel pipes, and sheets, alongside various construction materials like cement and concrete blocks. Their extensive distribution network ensures accessibility to customers in both urban and rural areas, catering to projects of all scales. The company's dedication to quality is paramount. J S Traders sources its products from reputable manufacturers and maintains rigorous quality control measures, guaranteeing materials that meet or exceed industry standards. This commitment has solidified their status as a trusted supplier. Customer satisfaction is at the forefront of their business philosophy. A dedicated team of experts assists clients in choosing the right materials for their projects, coupled with efficient and punctual deliveries, ensuring projects stay on track and earning the loyalty of their clientele. J S Traders has cultivated enduring partnerships in the construction industry through ethical practices and transparency. Beyond business, they engage with the local community, actively participating in initiatives related to education, healthcare, and social welfare, reflecting their commitment to corporate responsibility. In summary, since its establishment in 1984, J S Traders has evolved into a pioneering and dependable distributor of steel products and construction materials in Tamil Nadu and Pondicherry. Their dedication to quality, customer satisfaction, and community engagement has been the bedrock of their success, reaffirming their position as a cornerstone of the region's construction industry.
